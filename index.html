About Us

Hay Springs Public School is proud to offer multiple programs for students of all ages,
including an elementary program, middle school program, middle school program,
and a high school program. Please find information for each of our programs below:


_____________________________________________________________________________

Our Preschool Program

We have a lot planned for preschool this year. Each day we will have outdoor and indoor play
and exploration time, group activities like story time, music and movement, as well as individual
activities like art. Each week we will have a different theme such as a letter of the alphabet,
season, holiday, and/or social skill that we will focus on in activities and explorations. Our
program operates Monday-Thursday, a morning session 8 to 12 noon and an afternoon session
12 noon to 4, the same days that K-12 is in session. Young students attend the morning
session. Students who will be eligible to attend Kindergarten in the fall attend the afternoon
session. (See School Year Calendar below for days in session) Students are not required to
be residents of Hay Springs School District #3 to attend preschool.

Our Elementary Program

Our elementary program consists of Kindergarten through 5th grades. Enrollment is not
open throughout the year unless the student has just become a resident of the district.
Out-of-district and out-of-state students may be accepted upon application, if application
is made prior to the beginning of the semester.

Our Middle School Program

Our middle school program consists of 6th, 7th, and 8th grades. Enrollment is not open
throughout the year unless the student has just become a resident of the district.
Out-of-district and out-of-state students may be accepted upon application, if application
is made prior to the beginning of the semester.

Our High School Program

Our high school program consists of 9th through 12th grades. Enrollment is not open
throughout the year unless the student has just become a resident of the district.
Out-of-district and out-of-state students may be accepted upon application, if application
is made prior to the beginning of the semester.
